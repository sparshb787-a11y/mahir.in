How We Work

MAHIR is built to help you invest with more clarity and less noise. Every research note, every screen, and every recommendation is designed to make your investment decisions better, simpler, and more confident.

We do not chase trends or push products. Our advisory and screener start with one question: will this help the client build wealth over time? That question drives our stock research, risk framework, portfolio reviews, and the way we communicate every investment idea.

What you read in our reports is backed by documented research. The strict risk checks, compliance reviews, and our refusal to recommend anything we would not own ourselves are what make the advice worth following. MAHIR is for investors who take the long term seriously and expect the same from their partner.

Our Approach

Advice, Refined Down To The Decision.

Every MAHIR engagement follows the same four steps, whether you're a first-time investor or an existing client exploring a new product.

01

We Listen First

Goals / risk comfort / time horizon

We begin by understanding what you actually want from your investments — how much risk you are comfortable with, and how long you plan to stay invested. No generic portfolios, no assumptions.

02

We Study The Options

Research / fundamentals / risk checks

Our team digs into companies, numbers, and market data before suggesting anything. We only recommend what we have actually researched and believe in.

03

We Explain Our Reasoning

Clear advice / full transparency

Every suggestion comes with a plain explanation of why we think it makes sense for you. You will always know what we recommended and the thinking behind it.

04

We Stay With You

Ongoing review / support / accountability

Our job does not end with a recommendation. We track how things go, review our calls honestly, and stay available to you for any questions — long after the first advice is given.

FAQ

Questions,
answered plainly.

The essentials about who we are, how we work, and what to expect before you speak with us.

What does MAHIR actually do?
We are a SEBI-registered investment advisory and market intelligence firm. We help long-term investors build portfolios grounded in original research, disciplined risk, and clear reasoning, not noise or short-term calls.
Are you registered with SEBI?
Yes. MAHIR Group Investment Advisers Private Limited operates a Separately Identifiable Investment Advisory Unit registered with SEBI (RIA Reg. No. INA000022668) and enlisted with BSE (No. 2526). Registration is valid from June 01, 2026, Perpetual.
Who is this for?
Individuals, families, and institutions who take investing seriously and want a research-led partner focused on process, transparency, and a long horizon rather than product-pushing.
How do you charge?
Fees are advisory-only and disclosed transparently before onboarding, in line with SEBI's IA regulations. We do not earn commissions or brokerage on client transactions.
What is MAHIR Screener?
MAHIR Screener is our market intelligence product | a research toolkit for filtering, comparing, and understanding listed companies. It complements the advisory practice and is available at mahirscreener.com.
How do I get in touch?
Write to us at admin@mahiradvisers.com or call +91-9084945151. You can also use the Contact page and we will get back within one business day.
